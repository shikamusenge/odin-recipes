RWANDA Traditional Food

This is a very simple meal but often eaten. The component are boiled sweet potetos cooked beans and fresh avocado. They are alot of avocado in Rwanda and they are eaten Regulary.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Avocado
  • sweet potetos

Steps to prepare RWANDA Traditional Food(Beans And avocado width boiled sweet potetos)

  1. prepare source pan, water, cassava, sweetpotetos, beans and knife
  2. cook beans
  3. if beans is ready peel sweet potetos
  4. wash them and add it to beans
  5. after 30 minutes you can remove it and take it
